The Marathon Victory
马拉松的胜利
📖 12 句 📚 13 词 🎙 天心小灵
1Emma Woods stood at the manor grounds, her heart racing before the marathon.
2"I'm fit for this," she whispered to herself, tying her running shoes tight.
3Emily Dyer and Naidu stood by the track, waving their hands to cheer.
4The whistle blew and Emma ran, feeling her breath quicken with every step.
5Around her, other runners dashed forward in the match, their feet pounding the grass.
6Emma felt her legs ache but she kept going, her fit body refusing to give up.
7"Go for it, Emma!" Naidu shouted, waving a bright yellow flag.
8Emma pushed harder, her legs pumping toward the finishing line like never before.
9"Hold your breath!" Naidu yelled as Emma surged ahead in the final stretch.
10The crowd erupted when Emma crossed the line first, their voices filling the air.
11"You did it! You did it!" Emily Dyer screamed, hugging Emma with tears.
12A gold medal hung around Emma's neck as she smiled, her hard work finally paid off.
1艾玛·伍兹站在庄园的场地上,马拉松比赛前她的心跳加速。
2"我为此做好了准备,"她低声对自己说,把跑鞋系紧。
3艾米莉·戴尔和奈杜站在赛道旁,挥手欢呼。
4哨声响起,艾玛开始跑,每一步都感觉到呼吸加快。
5她周围,其他参赛者冲了出去,脚踩在草地上发出重击声。
6艾玛感到腿酸痛,但她继续前进,她健壮的身体拒绝放弃。
7"加油,艾玛!"奈杜喊道,挥舞着一面鲜黄的旗帜。
8艾玛更加用力推近,双腿以前所未有的力量向终点线冲去。
9"屏住呼吸!"奈杜在最后一段喊道,艾玛冲到了前面。
10当艾玛第一个冲过终点线时,人群沸腾了,声音充满了空气。
11"你做到了!你做到了!"艾米莉·戴尔哭着尖叫,紧紧拥抱着艾玛。
12一枚金牌挂在艾玛的脖子上,她微笑着,她的努力终于得到了回报。
