The Magical Chocolate Factory
神奇的巧克力工厂
📖 14 句 📚 16 词 🎙 天心小灵
1Doctor Emily Dyer arrived in the small town and smelled sweet chocolate everywhere.
2Kreacher Pierson owned a magical factory that made the most wonderful chocolate in the world.
3"Welcome to my factory!" Kreacher said with a warm smile.
4Emily walked inside and saw rivers of chocolate flowing like magic.
5It was the most exciting experience she had ever seen.
6Rich customers came from far away to buy the famous chocolate.
7But poor village children stood outside, watching with empty eyes.
8Emily's heart ached when she saw the hungry children.
9"We have so much chocolate, but they have nothing," she said.
10Kreacher smiled and believed every child deserved some joy.
11He filled a basket with chocolate and walked outside to the children.
12The children received chocolate and their faces became bright with surprise and happiness.
13Emily finally understood that wealth meant nothing without sharing with others.
14She left the magical factory feeling that everything good began with kindness.
