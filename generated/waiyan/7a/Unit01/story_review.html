A Strange Winter Day
一个奇异的冬日
📖 8 句 📚 5 词 🎙 天心小灵
1Emma Woods woke to a freezing, sunless morning, the sky gray as ash.
2A princess from a distant kingdom had taken shelter at the manor during the strange winter.
3They lit candles and began to play chess by the frosted window.
4"Your moves are clever," the princess whispered, her breath visible in the cold air.
5Emma poured two cups of watery soup, hoping the warmth would lift their spirits.
6The soup tasted thin, but the princess smiled anyway, grateful for the company.
7Outside, snow piled high against the walls while they waited for spring to arrive.
8"Spring will come," Emma said softly, "and so will the sun again."

4"你的棋步真巧妙,"公主轻声说道,她的呼吸在冷空气中清晰可见。
5艾玛倒了两杯稀薄的汤,希望温暖能提振她们的精神。
6汤尝起来味道很淡,但公主还是微笑着,感激有她作伴。
7外面,雪在墙边堆积如山,而她们等待着春天的到来。
8"春天会来的,"艾玛轻声说,"太阳也会再升起的。"
