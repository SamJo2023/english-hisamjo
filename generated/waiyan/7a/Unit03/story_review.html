At the Manor
在庄园
📖 9 句 📚 10 词 🎙 天心小灵
1The sun set over the manor, turning the dining room golden.
2Orpheus, Serveran, and Naidu sat around a plain wooden table, sharing warm dessert.
3"Iranian families store warm memories in sweets for celebrations," Orpheus said softly.
4Serveran nodded and offered a Western tale about a hero crossing twin bridges.
5"Do you think those bridges are dangerous?" Naidu asked, looking at the river below.
6"Some bridges are dangerous," Orpheus warned, "but they bridge our worlds together."
7The candles flickered softly on the table, casting dancing shadows across the walls.
8Serveran smiled at Naidu. "Tell us a tale from your culture next time."
9The evening remained warm and safe as they finished their dessert together.
1夕阳西下,庄园的餐厅染成了金色。
2奥菲斯、瑟维兰和奈杜围坐在一张朴素的木桌旁,一起分享着温热的甜点。
3奥菲斯轻声说:"伊朗家庭会把温暖的回忆贮藏在甜点里,用于庆祝。"
4瑟维兰点点头,讲了一个西方的故事,讲述一位英雄穿越双桥。
5奈杜看着桥下的河水,问道:"你觉得那些桥危险吗?"
6奥菲斯警告说:"有些桥是危险的,但它们把我们的世界连接在一起。"
7桌上的蜡烛轻轻摇曳,在墙上投下舞动的影子。
8瑟维兰对奈杜微笑着说:"下次给我们讲讲你们文化的故事吧。"
9大家一起吃完甜点,夜色依然温暖而安全。
